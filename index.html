Our Products

Explore our finest range of natural and refined salt

Sea Salt

Naturally harvested from seawater through solar evaporation, our Sea Salt is rich in essential minerals and perfect for culinary and industrial applications.

Lake Salt

Extracted from mineral-rich salt lakes, our Lake Salt is known for its purity and smooth texture, ideal for food-grade and commercial use.

Vacuum Powder Salt

Produced using advanced vacuum evaporation technology, this fine-grain salt ensures consistency, superior quality, and perfect solubility for industrial processing.

Refined Salt

High-purity refined salt, carefully processed and iodized for safe and healthy consumption. Ideal for everyday cooking and seasoning.
